You need a device (laptop, tablet, or phone) with internet, a camera, and microphone. We use Microsoft Teams for one-on-one classes.

We offer flexible timings to suit your schedule. Once you register, we will coordinate with you to fix convenient slots.

Yes. We have dedicated tutors for kids and offer courses like Qaida Noorania and Kids Islamic Studies in an engaging way.
